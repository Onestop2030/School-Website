Annual activities Charity Marathon

We participate in every year in marathon to raise funds to facilitate the needy student in the school. Taking lead is our director. The races include the 45km Elite race, 21km ultra race and the 15km children race

Students retreat Hiking tours

Our school is situated near Mt.Kenya game reserve, the Aberdare ranges, Mt kenya resort and many other tourist destination where we take our students every year.
